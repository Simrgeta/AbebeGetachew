Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 26, 2025

1. Information Collection

ABMS collects no personal data by default. Your database backups are encrypted locally before any cloud upload.

2. Dropbox Integration

If you choose to upload backups to Dropbox, ABMS only accesses the files you explicitly select. We do not collect, store, or share your Dropbox credentials. OAuth 2 authentication ensures secure access tokens are stored on your device.

3. Data Security

All database files are encrypted using AES encryption before leaving your device. ABMS never transmits unencrypted data.

4. Third-Party Services

Dropbox is the only third-party service integrated. ABMS adheres to Dropbox API security guidelines.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with the effective date updated.

By using ABMS, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
